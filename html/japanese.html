What is the JLPT?

The JLPT, or Japanese Language Proficiency Test (日本語能力試験), is a Japanese language test for non-native speakers used to certify ones knowledge in the language. Japanese companies and universities acknowledge it as a standard for gaging an individuals ability. The JLPT has five different levels ranging from the N5 (lowest), to the N1 (highest).
